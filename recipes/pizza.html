Pizza

Description

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Peperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make dough
  2. Roll out dough
  3. Add tomato sauce to dough
  4. Add toppings
  5. Bake
